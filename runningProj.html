Endless Strides: A Journey Through My Love for Long Distance Running

DeJon Johnson. April 11th, 2023. Webdroid Spring Cohort.

Running has been a part of human life since the beginning of time. It is a way to escape, connect with nature, and push one's limits. Distance running, in particular, has been my passion for many years. What started as just the races I was begrudgingly assigned when I ran track in middle school, has now become an integral part of my life.

I was introduced to running in my elementary days, where I was trained on various breathing techniques and the benefits running offers. Over time, I witnessed the way it helped me stay focused, determined, and disciplined. Witnessing these benefits firsthand, I became passionate about it.

The inspiration to continue this passion came from some of the most famous long-distance runners such as Emil Zatopek, Paula Radcliffe, and Eliud Kipchoge. Their accomplishments in the sport, like Kipchoge running a sub 2 hour marathon, continue to motivate me to push harder and strive to hit new fitness goals. I also find inspiration in everyday runners, who I run into on my runs, who go out there every day and run, regardless of their fitness levels or running goals.
